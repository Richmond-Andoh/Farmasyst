Products & Services

Our comprehensive and diverse platform, for effective farm and resource monitoring.

Monitoring and Supervison
monitoring software (SaaS) as a service for all Agribusinesses with our customized platform to monitor all farm activities and farmers and providing well trained agric extension officers and supervisors to guide, train and monitor farmers through real time farm alerts and reports.

Disease detection and Reporting
Provides an offline plant disease detection and a real time farm disease and emergency report and response system.

Farm Mapping and Tracking
Our system allows for the location of farms to be tracked to aid quick response assistance and monitoring services.
